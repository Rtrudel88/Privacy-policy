Privacy Policy for MemoirCode Database

Last updated: November 29th, 2023

Welcome to MemoirCode Database, owned and operated by UltimateAppsCA ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide when using our mobile application.

1. Information Collected:

  • User Account Information: We collect your email address for account creation and login purposes.
  • Order Information: If you make purchases through the app, we collect your address to fulfill and deliver orders.
  • Optional User Content: The app allows you to upload and store music, videos, and images at your discretion. This content is associated with your account.

2. How We Use Your Information:

  • Account Management: Your email address is used for account creation, authentication, and communication.
  • Order Fulfillment: We use your address to process and deliver orders made through the app.
  • User Content: Music, videos, and images are stored securely and are accessible only by you.

3. Sharing of Information:

  • We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties.
  • Employee Access: We may disclose user data to any member of our organization who reasonably needs access to user data to achieve the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy.

4. Data Security:

  • We employ industry-standard security measures, including encrypted cloud storage, to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration.

5. How Long We Store Personal Data:

  • User data will be stored until the purpose the data was collected for has been achieved. You will be notified if your data is kept for longer than this period.

6. Your Rights as a User:

  • Under the GDPR, you have the following rights:
    • Right to be informed
    • Right of access
    • Right to rectification
    • Right to erasure
    • Right to restrict processing
    • Right to data portability
    • Right to object

7. Children:

  • We do not knowingly collect or use personal data from children under 16 years of age. If we learn that we have collected personal data from a child under 16 years of age, the personal data will be deleted as soon as possible. If a child under 16 years of age has provided us with personal data, their parent or guardian may contact our privacy officer.

8. How to Access, Modify, Delete, or Challenge the Data Collected:

  • If you would like to know if we have collected your personal data, how we have used your personal data, if we have disclosed your personal data, and to whom we disclosed your personal data, or if you would like your data to be deleted or modified in any way, please contact our privacy officer at privacy@memoircodes.life.

9. Do Not Track Notice:

  • Do Not Track ("DNT") is a privacy preference that you can set in certain web browsers. We do not track the users of our app over time and across third party websites and therefore do not respond to browser-initiated DNT signals.

10. Modifications:

  • This Privacy Policy may be amended from time to time to maintain compliance with the law and reflect changes to our data collection process. When we amend this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this Privacy Policy. We recommend that users periodically review our Privacy Policy to stay informed of any updates.

11. Complaints:

If you feel we have not addressed your concern in a satisfactory manner, you may contact a supervisory authority.

12. Contact Information:
